Services

We provide measurement and analysis systems for testing and data validation of autonomous vehicle systems, and vision AI development to the world's leading OEM, tier1, and the vehicle driving platform industry.

ADAS Measurement Analysis System

Recording, re-processing, and analysis functions are essential in order to develop and test the autonomous system and vision AI systems

Accelerator Design Service

Provides accelerator design to operate vision algorithms for robots, medical systems, vision AI and autonomous vehicles in real time.

Display Drive Controller Module

Our DDC technology provides drive performance for high frame rate and high-resolution displays, including panels, optical technologies and video processors.

ASIC (System on Chip) Design

Using our vast experience and technology, we serve to develop SoC chips that perfectly fit customers needs.

ADAS Chips

For the development and testing of vision AI systems (including autonomous system)s, equipment for logging, reprocessing, HiLs and analysis of sensor data and network data for vehicles is absolutely necessary.

Applications include:

  • Sensor data: Camera (include stereo), Radar, Lidar, GPS, IMU, etc

  • Vehicle network data : CAN (FD), FlexRay, Automotive ethernet, etc

Based on sophisticated synchronization (minimum 12.8 ns) between data, our product meet the perfect HiLs condition without loss of data
